M&Ms Chocolate Cake

This M&Ms chocolate cake is the perfect birthday cake! It's so simple to make and looks amazing decorated with chocolate biscuits, frosting & M&Ms!

Ingredients:

  • 1 chocolate cake (see notes for the recipe I used)
  • 450 g chocolate frosting storebought or homemade
  • 400 g chocolate finger biscuits
  • 400 g M&Ms

Steps:

  1. Make the chocolate cake according to the instructions (or use a bought one). Allow to cool completely.
  2. Cover the top and sides of the cake with frosting.
  3. Decorate the sides of the cake with the chocolate finger biscuits.
  4. Add chocolate fingers biscuits to the top of the cake to act as dividers for the M&Ms.
  5. Place the M&Ms into the different sections of the cake.
  6. Add a small amount of frosting to the top of the chocolate fingers biscuits on the top of the cake and cover with extra M&Ms.
  7. Tie a ribbon around the outside of the cake.
